6. Median
The median is the middle number in a sorted, ascending or descending, list of numbers and can be more descriptive of that data set than the average.
The median is sometimes used as opposed to the mean when there are outliers in the sequence that might skew the average of the values.

8. HCF and LCM
Factors: All the number that divides the number completely. i.e. without leaving any remainder are called the factor of the number.
LCM: The smallest positive number that is a multiple of two or more numbers.
HCF: The greatest number that is a factor of two (or more) other numbers.
